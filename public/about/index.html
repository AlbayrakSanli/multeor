Multeor

Multeor is a multiplayer webgame developed by Arjen de Vries, Filidor Wiese and Arthur van 't Hoog. You control a meteor crashing into earth and score points by leaving the biggest trail of destruction.

How to play

First, set up your desktop computer as game viewer (on which the game itself is shown), then, sync your mobile device(s) to use as a controller. You can play it solo, but we recommend challenging your friends or collegues to join in. The more players, the more chaos, colors and destruction!

Requirements

Multeor is a modern webgame using some of the latest techniques, like Canvas and Websockets. To enjoy the game to the fullest, please make sure your desktop computer and mobile devices meet the following criteria:

Mobile device requirements

  • iOS 5.2+ (and iPhone 3Gs or higher)
  • Android 4.0.2+ (with Chrome or Firefox)
  • Multitouch support

Desktop requirements

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Internet Explorer 10+
