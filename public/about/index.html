About Multeor

It started out as a side-project for us, something we always wanted to but never got around to do. When the local indie game-jam Gameleon took place, we took it upon ourselves to finally go for it. Screw paying customers, we thought, and we lived hungerly ever after...

Requirements

Multeor is a modern webgame using some of the latest techniques, like Canvas and Websockets. To enjoy the game to the fullest, please make sure your desktop computer and mobile devices meet the following criteria:

Mobile device requirements

  • iOS 5.2+ (and iPhone 3Gs or higher)
  • Android 4.0.2+ (with Chrome or Firefox)
  • Multitouch support

Desktop requirements

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Internet Explorer 10+ (9 works but is slow)
